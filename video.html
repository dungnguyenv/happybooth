Le' Film

At Happy Booth — Studio, we have always had a deep passion for creating wedding films that are not just ordinary recordings, but a timeless documentary of your day. We strive to capture the genuine emotions and in-between moments that unfold throughout each couple’s special day.

Our goal is not only to document the events of the day but to capture both the big and the small moments that make each wedding truly special. We understand the importance of preserving memories.

With our unique cinematic documentary style, we aim to create films that go beyond traditional wedding videos. We blend artistic storytelling techniques, beautiful cinematography, each film is carefully edited, color-graded, and infused with a tailored musical score, ensuring that every frame resonates with the love and emotion felt on the day.
