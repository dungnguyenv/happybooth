Est. 2012

Over the years, Happy Booth — Photobooth Event has grown into a cohesive team of event specialists. What started as a small setup has evolved into a premier photobooth service, where our shared passion for bringing fun to events is the driving force behind it all. Each team member brings their unique skills, experiences, and creative perspective to ensuring your guests have an unforgettable time.

Our growth as a photobooth rental company allows us to provide comprehensive coverage for weddings, birthdays, and corporate events. We ensure that each client receives the utmost care, high-quality prints, and beautiful custom frames. Together, we are committed to providing the ultimate entertainment experience for you to hold onto forever.
