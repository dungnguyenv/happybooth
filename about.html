HB — Studio

Est. 2012

Over the years, Happy Booth — Studio has grown from a duo into a cohesive team of talented individuals. What started as a partnership has evolved into a close-knit studio, where our shared passion for capturing the beauty of weddings is the driving force behind it all. Each team member brings their unique skills, experiences and creative perspective to the table — allowing us to deliver timeless galleries & films.

Our growth as a studio team not only allows us to provide comprehensive coverage of weddings, but also ensures that each couple receives the utmost care and attention to detail. Together, we are committed to documenting the precious moments of your day in creating timeless memories for our couples to hold.

FAQs

Whilst we are from New Zealand — we are primarily based in the UK, covering weddings across the UK and Europe for most of the year. However, during the colder months from late November to March, we head down south to New Zealand and Australia. If you're planning a wedding outside of these regions, feel free to reach out — we're accustomed to traveling and often take on projects in other locations.

Our style of capturing a wedding is one of which a much more candid and timeless approach is taken. We love to shoot in a way that tells a story of the day through not just the big moments but has a focus also on little, often overseen moments that occur throughout. We have a large focus on the people, details & surroundings, paired with the art of capturing in a timeless manner. It's important to note that our larger galleries displayed on our website showcase much more of our couples and leave out many of the more personal moments between them and their loved ones for our couples privacy, but each gallery our couples receive after their wedding day contain a significant amount of imagery highlighting their day filled with their loved ones and all that comes with.
